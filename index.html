Hello There!

This is my food website! It's mostly a repository for recipes, a promise to my friend Tim who was always asking for good things to cook. Eventually I got fed up and wanted to make a website where he could go to find every recipe I have rather than have him ask me (I love you Tim).

This site is pretty barebones and is 90% recipes but does include my pizza process and opinions on pizza places I've been to. The recipes are not all mine, and are really just tried and true recipes that I have used before and trust will make a solid meal. Feel free to steal and modify as you see fit.

Most of my fun stuff happens on MacFlora, a constant process that was originally plants but has devolved into other things. Thanks for visiting!
